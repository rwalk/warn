About us

The mission of OpenDoxie is to assemble and showcase unique datasets that give alternative viewpoints on important business and social welfare topics. Our WARN notification database is our first public beta product. We've giving open access to this database in a firendly, searchable format and supporting our operational costs through advertising.

Our data products are also available for private purchase. Please contact us for details. In keeping with our mission, researchers and non-profits can obtain the data for a nominal processing fee.
